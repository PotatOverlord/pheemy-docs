## Concept
A second chance for the traitors, who only gets their true mask when their troupe is wiped out.

## Abilities
When all members of the Traitor troupe have died, this mask transforms into a traitor mask determined by the current [Masquerade](../../masquerades.md).

## Gameplay
The sleeper agent is one of the few roles that changes how they're seen over the course of the game, with their presence being a wrench in the gears for potential oracles and the likes.

The sleeper agent wins with their current troupe, so if the traitors don't **all** die, they still win with the crew. This is not necessarily traditional to the werewolf role (aptly named [Traitor](https://werewolf.chat/Traitor)) this mask is based on, but is necessary due to the outstanding impact even a [Recruit](recruit.md) can have on the game.

They give the traitors a second shot if they get wiped, by resuming where they left off, but the traitors need to leave off in a strong position for the sleeper agent to do particularly well. Missing only the nuke objective (for example) is entirely recoverable.

Upon conversion, the sleeper agent is given a cache location for their new mask and an audio cue to inform them of the change.
